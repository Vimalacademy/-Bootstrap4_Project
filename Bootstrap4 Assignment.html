About the Game

"Assassin's Creed Valhalla" is an action-adventure video game set during the Viking Age in the 9th century AD. Players take on the role of Eivor, a Viking raider from Norway, leading a clan of Vikings in search of a new home in England. Facing off against Anglo-Saxon kingdoms, Eivor forges alliances and interacts with historical figures, including the legendary Viking hero, Ragnar Lothbrok. The game features an expansive open-world environment, parkour-based movement, stealth mechanics, and a narrative that weaves Viking history with Assassin's Creed lore. Released in November 2020.
